The Double Education of My Twins' Chinese School

After years of teaching, the school finally achieved recognition for its unique approach to bilingual education and cultural preservation in the community.

Published in the print edition of the July 3, 2023, issue, with the headline "Double Education."

The program expanded rapidly across multiple districts and became a model for other communities seeking similar educational approaches.

Originally published in the spring 2024 special edition with additional commentary from the editorial board.
